Enrollment Guidelines
Do's:
  • Ensure the student is looking directly at the camera
  • Make sure there's adequate lighting on the face
  • Remove glasses, hats, or masks if possible
  • Keep a neutral expression
  • Ensure only one person is in the camera frame
  • Stand at arm's length from the camera
Don'ts:
  • Don't enroll with poor lighting or shadows
  • Don't have multiple people in the frame
  • Don't use blurry or unclear images
  • Don't enroll the same person twice
  • Don't tilt your head too much
  • Don't stand too close or too far from camera
Note: Better enrollment photos lead to more accurate attendance tracking. Take time to ensure quality enrollment images.
